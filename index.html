Legal Framework

Governed by Acts (WHS Act 2011, Protection from Harmful Radiation Act 1990), Regulations (WHS Regulation 2017), Codes of Practice, Australian Standards, and workplace policies.

Rights & Duties

Employers ensure safety, workers follow procedures, and both have legal responsibilities.

"Reasonably Practicable" & "Due Diligence"

Core concepts of practical and effective safety measures.

WHS Authorities

SafeWork NSW enforces laws, advises, and penalizes non-compliance.

Other Legislation

Includes Health Practitioner Act, Poisons and Therapeutic Goods Act, Health Records and Information Privacy Act, and Radiation Control Act for healthcare safety.
